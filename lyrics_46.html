He displays many amorous pastimes inthe groves of Vrindavana, He is the lover of the cowherd maidens of Vraja, the holder of the great hill namedGovardhana, the beloved son of mother Yasoda, the delighter of the inhabitants of Vraja, and He wandersin the forests along the banks of the River Yamuna. (Srila Prabhupada was very fond of this song and sang it justbefore his lectures.
