Sri Nrisimha Pranama


namaste narasimhaya
prahladahlada-dayine
hiranyakasiporvakshah
sila-tanka-nakhalaye
I offer my obeisances to Lord Narasimha who gives joy to PrahladaMaharaja and whose nails are like chisels on the stonelike chest of the demon Hiranyakasipu. 

ito nrisimhah parato nrisimho
yato yato yami tato nrisimhah
bahir nrisimho hridaye nrisimho
nrisimham adim saranam prapadye
Lord Nrisimha is here and also there. Wherever I go Lord Nrisimhais there. He is in the heart and is outside as well. I surrender to Lord Nrisimha, the origin of all things andthe supreme refuge. 

tava kara-kamala-vare nakhamadbhuta-sringam
dalita-hiranyakasipu-tanu-bhringam
kesava dhrita-narahari-rupa jaya jagadisa hare
O Kesava! O Lord of the universe! O Lord Hari, who have assumed theform of half-man, half-lion! All glories to You!
